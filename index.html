About Me

Aspiring full stack developer with a background in nonprofit administration. I have a Masters in Business Administration (MBA) with a Marketing focus from the University of Massachusetts Boston. I lived abroad as a full-time volunteer in Costa Rica and Dominican Republic working with community leaders to help underprivileged youth. I love to travel, and became fluent in Spanish during my time in Central America. Other fun facts? I am a crazy dog mom who likes to knit in my spare time. My Goal: to use the skills I will be developing in the University of Arizona Coding Bootcamp to to work for a company with a socially responsible mission, and help make the world a better place.
